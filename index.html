What is BehaviorSelf?

Maybe you’ve told yourself that this is THE year I’m going to …, and then nothing happens. Nothing long lasting anyways.

When you’ve previously attempted to create change in your life, from losing weight, getting more fit, changing the way you manage your life or seeking to change your mindset, you have probably discovered that the quick fixes only give you a short-term thrill while you white-knuckle it on your intensive and restrictive plan. But then what happens? You slowly revert back to your old ways. Because you’ve trained your brain to do things in a certain way, it’s so common to revert back to those old patterns.

But your past doesn’t have to equal your future. Change is possible!

Join our vibrant and compassionate BehaviorSelf community for our weekly sessions discussing topics relevant to developing our future thoughts, feelings and actions, as well as our monthly Emotional Eating Workshops.

Envision a future shaped by the decisions you make today. Picture yourself achieving the success you've worked towards, a reality crafted by your own hands.

And, I’m here to support you every step of the way. Contact me for a free trial session. I can’t wait meet and support you!
